The ParkProCura Story

A new parking experience in Edmonton has arrived. Introducing ParkProCura – Edmonton’s newest parking service. Founded in 2018, ParkProCura is a division of ProCura Real Estate Services, the award-winning real estate development and management company, and currently manages a diverse parking portfolio of both premium surface lots and parkades. With locations including the downtown Edmonton core and Century Park, ParkProCura has got Edmonton parkers covered. Get in touch (this will be a link to the contact form, once created) today to experience the ParkProCura difference.

ProCura Real Estate Services

ProCura Real Estate Services is an award-winning real estate development and management company whose mission is to elevate people’s quality of life.

Since 1979, ProCura’s forward-thinking projects have redefined skylines and the urban living experience across western Canada. The company does it through a unique ability to envision, develop, and reimagine spaces – thoughtfully connecting them to the lifestyles of the people who inhabit them. Its signature: Advanced sustainability driven by innovative technology.

Notable recent projects include: the reimagining of WSP Place, corporate home to an A-list group of companies, including one of the world’s largest engineering firms; the award-winning LEED® GOLD-CS Certified Intact Insurance Building; The Mayfair on Jasper luxury apartments; and the visionary mixed-use development now taking shape at Century Park. And that’s just the beginning.

ProCura’s future project pipeline consists of multiple urban locations in Edmonton, encompassing over 1.4 million square feet of land holdings, over 4.4 million square feet of developable space, and over 6,900 multi-family units.
